G20 Summit 2023 — Award-Winning Poster

Visual Design

The poster explores how society struggles to leverage its local potential and identity — through poverty, public spaces, and economic disparity, and how these manifest in the digital world. While delivery platforms and streaming services have become symbols of comfort and convenience, a large part of the population still struggles to afford basic necessities. The work raises awareness of that disparity and the urgent need to address it. Third winning entry in a national poster-making competition.
